Wordle

How to Play:

  • Start typing. The letters will appear
  • Remove letters with Backspace
  • Hit Enter/Return to submit an answer
  • Letters with green background are in the right spot
  • Letters with yellow background exist in the world, but are in the wrong spots
  • Letters with gray background do not exist in the world
  • If you need a hint, click the ? icon
